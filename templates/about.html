Know your Aadhar Card

What is Aadhaar?

Aadhaar links demographic and biometric data of individuals, enabling easy access to various government and private services.

How to Read Your Aadhaar?

Your Aadhaar card includes personal details like name, DOB, gender, address, and your Aadhaar number. The QR code contains encoded data for verification.

Security of Aadhaar

Your Aadhaar data is securely stored and protected. Always share it responsibly and avoid sharing Aadhaar numbers publicly.

How to Update Aadhaar?

You can update your Aadhaar details online or at authorized Aadhaar centers by submitting valid documents.

Learn More About Aadhaar

To learn more or download your e-Aadhaar, visit the official UIDAI website

Aadhaar and Address Proof

Aadhaar serves as a valid address proof for applying to various services, including passports, driving licenses, and voter IDs.
